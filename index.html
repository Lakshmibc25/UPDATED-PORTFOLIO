Introduction

About

Remesh VP is a retired Indian Army officer and defence quality assurance professional based in Bengaluru, Karnataka. He holds an M.Tech in Mechanical Engineering from Jawaharlal Nehru University, an MBA in HR from IGNOU, and an MBA in Finance from Dayananda Sagar Institutions, along with a Diploma in Quality Assurance from Bharathiar University.

He is currently serving as Senior Quality Assurance Officer (SQAO) with DGQA at Ordnance Factory Varangaon, where his work covers inspection, proof testing, and certification of ammunition and defence stores. His professional focus has been on maintaining compliance with ISO/IEC 17020:2012 standards and coordinating between manufacturing units, R&D agencies, and operational users.

Beyond his defence career, he is a certified Art of Living teacher since 2007, a Six Sigma Black Belt holder, and holds the PTS certification. He is also a Board Member at Kendriya Vidyalaya Varangaon and supports Indian MSME initiatives through ViratBharat Enterprises.
